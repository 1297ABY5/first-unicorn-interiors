Privacy Policy

How First Unicorn Interiors collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: February 2026

Who We Are

First Unicorn Interiors ("we", "us", "our") is a Dubai-based design, renovation and construction company operating under First Unicorn Group. Our registered business address is Regus, The Bridge Building, Sports City, Dubai, UAE.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a consultation request via our website form
  • Contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email
  • Request a free site visit or design consultation
  • Use our renovation cost calculator

This information may include your name, phone number, email address, villa location, project requirements, and budget range.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide project consultations
  • Prepare quotes and design proposals for your renovation project
  • Communicate project updates during active renovations
  • Send follow-up communications about your project interest
  • Improve our website and services

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as required to deliver our services (for example, sharing project details with our in-house team members working on your villa).

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics and similar tools to understand how visitors interact with our pages. These tools collect anonymous browsing data such as pages visited, time on site, and referral source. No personally identifiable information is collected through these tools.

WhatsApp Communication

When you contact us via WhatsApp, your messages are processed through the WhatsApp platform. Please refer to WhatsApp's own privacy policy for how your data is handled within their platform. We only use the information you share with us on WhatsApp for the purposes you intended — responding to your enquiry and managing your project.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy. Project-related data may be retained for warranty and legal compliance purposes.
