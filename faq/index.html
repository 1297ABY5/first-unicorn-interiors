Costs & Pricing

A full villa renovation ranges from AED 150,000 to AED 1,500,000+, depending on villa size, scope, material quality, and community. A 3-bed refresh starts around AED 150,000. A luxury 5-bed transformation in Palm Jumeirah or Emirates Hills can exceed AED 1,000,000. Kitchen renovations start from AED 45,000 and bathrooms from AED 35,000. Material quality is the biggest factor — Italian marble costs 3-4x more than quality porcelain. Other drivers: scope (cosmetic vs structural), villa size, design complexity, and whether the company uses vague "allowances" or itemises every material. Always ask for a line-by-line breakdown. If a quote doesn't specify exact materials, you're comparing apples to oranges.

Approvals & Permits

Most renovations require a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the developer and sometimes Dubai Municipality approval. Structural modifications, facade changes, and pool works always require permits. We handle all of this as part of our service — no additional charge, no headaches for you.
