Sobha Realty Standard Finishes: What You Get & How to Personalise

Sobha Realty is known for premium construction quality and attention to detail — they are one of the few UAE developers that handles both development and construction in-house. Their villas typically feature higher-than-average fit-out standards. This checklist helps you identify personalisation opportunities in your Sobha home.

Sobha delivers homes with a solid foundation of quality finishes. This guide walks through what is included as standard and the most popular ways homeowners choose to personalise each area to suit their lifestyle.

Communities

Sobha Hartland, Sobha One, Sobha Reserve

Room-by-Room Finishes Guide

Kitchen — What's Included & Personalisation Options

Sobha kitchens are generally well-specified with quality cabinetry, stone countertops, and integrated appliance provisions. The standard of finish is notably higher than many competitors. Personalisations tend to focus on appliance upgrades to premium European brands, custom pantry additions, and enhanced lighting design.

Bathrooms — What's Included & Personalisation Options

Sobha bathrooms come with quality fixtures, well-laid tiles, and good waterproofing. The fit-out is above average for the market. Popular personalisations include upgrading to designer fixtures, adding underfloor heating, and installing statement lighting and mirrors.

Flooring — What's Included & Personalisation Options

Flooring is typically well-chosen with good quality materials. Sobha is known for using real marble in key areas. Personalisations often focus on adding engineered wood in bedrooms or upgrading to book-matched marble in formal living spaces.

Electrical & Smart Home — What's Included & Personalisation Options

Electrical systems are modern and well-specified. Sobha homes are generally well-prepared for smart home integration. Common personalisations include full automation systems, integrated AV, and motorised blinds.

Outdoor & Landscaping — What's Included & Personalisation Options

Outdoor areas vary by project. Sobha Hartland villas often have direct park or water views. Pool installation, landscape lighting, and outdoor living areas complement the premium indoor finishes.

What Homeowners Typically Invest in Personalisation

AED 100,000 to AED 800,000+ — the higher base specification means personalisations tend to focus on premium upgrades rather than basic improvements.
