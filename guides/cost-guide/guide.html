8. How to Read a Renovation Quote

Green Flags ‚úÖ

  • Itemised material specifications (brand, model, colour code)
  • Separate labour and material costs visible
  • Milestone-based payment schedule (not more than 30% upfront)
  • Timeline with specific start and completion dates
  • Warranty terms in writing
  • Company trade licence number on the quote

Red Flags üö©

  • "Material allowance" instead of specific products ‚Äî they'll upsell later
