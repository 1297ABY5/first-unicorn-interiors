Property Inspection Checklist for The Lakes

After 34+ renovation projects in The Lakes, these are the things we always wish buyers had checked before purchasing:

  1. Inspect waterproofing on ground floor and around any lake-facing walls ‚Äî moisture damage is the number one hidden cost in The Lakes
  2. Check the age and condition of the HVAC system ‚Äî original units are well past their efficient lifespan
  3. Look for signs of tile lifting in bathrooms, which indicates failed waterproofing beneath
  4. Verify the electrical panel meets current DEWA safety standards
  5. Assess the pool equipment age if the villa has one ‚Äî pump and filter replacements run AED 15,000-25,000
  6. Check boundary wall condition ‚Äî many are showing cracks from settlement over the years
