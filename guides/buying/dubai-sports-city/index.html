Should You Buy in Dubai Sports City?

An honest guide to buying property in Dubai Sports City, Dubai — including the renovation costs nobody tells you about.

Quick Facts

Developer: Dubai Properties
Villa Types: 3BR to 5BR villas and townhouses
Age: 2010–2018, established community
Active Listings: 56+
Renovation Budget: AED 80,000–350,000

The Community

A sports-themed community surrounding the Dubai International Cricket Stadium and Els Club golf course. Victory Heights and other villa clusters offer family-friendly living with a strong community feel. Regular sporting events and well-maintained green spaces create an active lifestyle atmosphere.

Renovation Reality Check

Sports City villas are 8-15 years old now — prime renovation time. Budget AED 80K-350K depending on scope.

Based on our experience working in Dubai Sports City, here are the most common renovation needs:

Mid-age renovation needs

Properties from 2010-2015 are hitting the renovation sweet spot — old enough to need updating but structurally sound.

Kitchen reconfiguration

Closed kitchens with breakfast counters were the standard design. Full open-plan conversions with islands are the most requested upgrade.

Pool renovation

Existing pools from the original build often need retiling, equipment upgrades, and surround renovation after 8-10 years.

Outdoor living expansion

Large gardens perfect for covered terraces, BBQ areas, and children's play zones with artificial turf.

What We'd Budget

For a typical villa in Dubai Sports City, renovation costs range from AED 80,000–350,000 depending on scope. Kitchen-only renovations start lower, while full villa transformations with pool and landscaping sit at the top end.
