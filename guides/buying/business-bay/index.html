Property Inspection Checklist for Business Bay

After 31+ renovation projects in Business Bay, these are the things we always wish buyers had checked before purchasing:

  1. Research the specific building thoroughly ‚Äî quality varies dramatically
  2. Check service charge levels and any special assessment history
  3. Verify the building management company's reputation
  4. Inspect for any water damage or damp issues
  5. Assess noise levels ‚Äî some locations face busy roads
  6. Review the building's contractor approval process before buying
