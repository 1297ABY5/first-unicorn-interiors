Honest Assessment

Pros & Cons of Buying in District One

✔ Why Buy Here

  • Crystal lagoon and private beaches — unmatched amenity
  • Ultra-exclusive gated community with high privacy
  • Modern construction with contemporary architecture
  • Strong capital appreciation trajectory
  • Central location between Downtown and Meydan

✘ Watch Out For

  • Premium pricing — entry level starts at AED 15M
  • Some community amenities still under development
  • Developer finishes may not match the price positioning
  • Limited retail within the community currently
  • Some plots face ongoing construction in surrounding areas

Before You Sign

Property Inspection Checklist for District One

After 12+ renovation projects in District One, these are the things we always wish buyers had checked before purchasing:

  1. Assess whether developer finishes match your expectations — many buyers upgrade immediately
  2. Check smart home infrastructure and pre-wiring capacity
  3. Inspect external cladding and facade materials quality
  4. Verify lagoon access rights and any associated fees
  5. Review community management fees — they can be substantial
  6. Check for any pending developer works or snag list items
After Purchase

What to Renovate First in District One

Based on our experience across 12+ projects in District One, here are the renovations that deliver the most value — both for daily living and resale:

  1. Interior finish customisation (marble, stone, wood) — AED 200,000-500,000
  2. Kitchen upgrade with imported European materials — AED 150,000-350,000
  3. Smart home system installation — AED 80,000-200,000
  4. Pool and outdoor entertainment area — AED 150,000-400,000
  5. Master suite renovation — AED 100,000-250,000
