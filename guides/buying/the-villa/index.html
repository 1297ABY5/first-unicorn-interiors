Lifestyle & Transport

What's It Like to Live Here?

Space-focused living for families who prioritise large homes and gardens over proximity to the city centre. The community has a quiet, residential character with less social infrastructure than Arabian Ranches.

Getting Around

Access via Al Ain Road and Hessa Street. 25 minutes to Downtown, 30 to Marina. Fully car dependent ‚Äî no metro access.

Schools Nearby

GEMS Founders School, Hartland International School, Nord Anglia (Dubai), South View School

Best For

Large families who want maximum space at an affordable price point and are prepared to invest in renovation. Excellent for those who want a villa they can truly customise to their taste.

What Buyers Should Know
