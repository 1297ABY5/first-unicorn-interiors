Kitchen renovation in Dubai costs between AED 25,000 and AED 180,000 depending on size, materials, and scope. What Drives Kitchen Renovation Cost in Dubai?

1. Cabinetry (40-50% of total cost)

Cabinetry is the single largest cost in any kitchen renovation. Flat-pack imported cabinets start around AED 800 per linear metre. Custom-built in-house joinery with soft-close hinges, push-to-open mechanisms, and handleless profiles costs AED 1,800 to AED 3,500 per linear metre. We build all our cabinetry in-house — no outsourcing, no middleman markup.

3. Appliances (10-20% of total cost)

Budget packages (Bosch/Siemens) run AED 8,000 to AED 15,000 for a full set. Premium packages (Miele, Gaggenau, Sub-Zero) can reach AED 40,000 to AED 80,000. We source direct from authorised UAE distributors — same warranty, better pricing.

4. Tiling and Splashback (5-10% of total cost)

Basic ceramic: AED 35-80 per sqm installed. Premium porcelain: AED 80-200 per sqm. Natural stone: AED 150-400 per sqm. Glass splashback: AED 200-500 per sqm.

5. Plumbing and Electrical (10-15% of total cost)

Moving a sink or adding a dishwasher connection adds AED 2,000-5,000. Full electrical rewiring for new lighting layout: AED 3,000-8,000. Under-cabinet LED lighting: AED 1,500-3,000.

Timeline: How Long Does a Kitchen Renovation Take?

A complete kitchen renovation in Dubai typically takes 3 to 6 weeks from demolition to handover. Budget refresh (doors, paint, handles) can be done in 1-2 weeks. Premium with structural changes: 6-8 weeks. Our in-house team handles demolition, plumbing, electrical, tiling, joinery, countertop installation, appliance fitting, and final styling — one team, one timeline, no coordination headaches.

Pro Tip: The cheapest way to transform a kitchen is replacing doors, handles, and countertops while keeping the existing layout. This avoids plumbing and electrical changes — saving AED 10,000-20,000 compared to a full refit.

What's NOT Included in Most Quotes (But Should Be)

Watch out for these hidden costs that other contractors leave out of their initial quote:

  • Demolition and disposal — AED 2,000-5,000. We include this.
  • Waterproofing — Essential behind sinks. AED 1,000-2,000. We include this.
  • Painting — After tiling, walls need repainting. AED 1,500-3,000. We include this.
  • Appliance installation — Some contractors quote supply only. We install everything.
  • Municipality NOC — Required for structural changes. We handle the paperwork.
