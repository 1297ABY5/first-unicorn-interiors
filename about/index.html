About
Our Story

From Construction Sites to
Commanding Projects

First Unicorn Group was founded after 15+ years in UAE construction — from hands-on site work to managing multi-million dirham projects. The vision was simple: build the company he wished existed when he was a homeowner.

One where every trade is in-house, every quote is honest, and every project is managed as if it were our own home.

Today, First Unicorn Interiors operates across four divisions — Design & Approvals, Interiors, Renovation, and Contracting — serving Dubai's most discerning homeowners and developers.
