Common Questions

Pool renovation starts from AED 40,000 for retiling and equipment. New pool construction ranges from AED 80,000 to AED 500,000+ depending on size, type, and features.

Pool renovation takes 4-8 weeks. New construction takes 8-12 weeks including filtration, tiling, decking, and commissioning.

Yes. We manage all community NOC applications, municipality permits, and engineering approvals at no extra cost.

Pool retiling starts from AED 25,000 for a standard residential pool. This includes draining, surface preparation, waterproofing, new tiles, grouting, and refilling. Glass mosaic tiles cost more than porcelain. We provide an exact quote after a free site visit.

October through March is ideal. Lower temperatures mean faster curing times and the pool is typically out of daily use during cooler months. Starting in October means your pool is ready for the winter swimming season. Summer renovation is possible but takes slightly longer.

If you have 3+ loose or cracked tiles, persistent water clarity issues despite chemical treatment, visible plumbing leaks, or your pool is over 12 years old with original tiles — renovation is likely more cost-effective than patching. We offer a free assessment where we inspect the pool and recommend the most cost-effective approach.
