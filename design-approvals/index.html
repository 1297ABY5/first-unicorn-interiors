Why It Matters

Every Successful Build Starts with the Right Drawings and Approvals

In Dubai, no construction project moves forward without the proper approvals. Whether you are renovating a villa in Arabian Ranches or building a new residence on Palm Jumeirah, architectural drawings, engineering documentation and municipality clearances form the bedrock of every project. Without them, contractors cannot price accurately, authorities will not issue permits, and timelines spiral out of control.

Our Design & Approvals division handles the entire journey — from the first concept sketch through to the final stamped permit. We work with Dubai Municipality, Trakhees, DDA, Nakheel and every relevant authority so you never have to chase paperwork, decode regulations or visit government offices yourself.

With over 800 projects approved across Dubai's most demanding communities, we know exactly what each authority requires, which common mistakes delay applications, and how to fast-track the process when timelines are tight.
