Why Choose Us

What Makes Us Different From Every Other Renovation Company in Dubai

Dubai has hundreds of companies calling themselves interior renovation contractors. Most of them operate the same way: a salesman takes your brief, a designer draws something up, and then the actual construction is handed to subcontractors who have never seen the design and have no relationship with you. The result is predictable — delays, quality issues, finger-pointing, and a finish that does not match what you approved.

We built First Unicorn Interiors specifically to solve this problem. Our electricians, plumbers, HVAC technicians, tilers, joiners, painters and stone installers are all employed directly. They work under one project manager, follow one programme, and communicate daily. When a joinery measurement needs confirming before the countertop is ordered, our joiner walks across the site and confirms it with our tiler. There is no email chain between three different companies. No finger-pointing. No gaps between trades.

This is not a minor operational detail — it is the single biggest factor in whether your renovation is delivered on time, on budget, and to the standard you were promised. Companies that subcontract every trade cannot guarantee coordination. We can, because the entire team answers to one project manager and one company.

Every quotation we issue is line-by-line. Every material is specified by brand, model and colour. Every payment is tied to a milestone — you only pay when work is completed, inspected and approved. There are no deposits demanded upfront for materials that may never arrive. There are no vague "allowances" that balloon into surprise costs halfway through the project.

Interior Renovation Company FAQ

How much does interior renovation cost in Dubai?

Interior renovation costs in Dubai range from AED 150 to AED 350+ per square foot depending on scope and material tier. A kitchen renovation starts from AED 35,000, a bathroom from AED 15,000, and a full villa renovation from AED 150,000. We provide transparent, line-by-line quotations with no hidden costs. Every item is itemised so you know exactly what you are paying for before work begins. Material specifications include brand, model and colour — no vague "allowances" that inflate during the project.

What is the best interior renovation company in Dubai?

The best renovation company is one that uses in-house teams rather than subcontractors, provides transparent pricing with itemised quotations, holds a valid Dubai Municipality licence, has verifiable project photos and reviews, and offers milestone-based payments. First Unicorn Interiors meets all these criteria with 800+ completed projects, 15+ years in the UAE, and a team of directly employed tradespeople covering every discipline from electrical and plumbing to joinery and painting. We encourage you to compare us against any other contractor using these criteria.

How long does a villa renovation take in Dubai?

A single-room renovation such as a kitchen or bathroom typically takes 4 to 8 weeks. A full villa renovation covering all rooms, flooring, painting and MEP works takes 3 to 6 months depending on villa size and specification level. We provide a detailed construction programme with milestones during the design phase. Milestone-based payments mean you only pay as work is completed and inspected. You receive daily WhatsApp photo updates so you can track progress without visiting site.

Do you provide free consultations and quotations?

Yes. We provide a free site visit and consultation for every project. During the visit we take measurements, discuss your requirements and budget, and assess the scope of work. Within 5 working days you receive a detailed quotation with line-by-line pricing for every item, a 3D design concept, and a proposed construction programme. There is no obligation and no cost for this process. If we are not the right fit, there are no hard feelings.

Do you handle municipality approvals and NOCs for renovation?

Yes. Many renovation projects in Dubai require a No Objection Certificate from the community developer or building management, and some require municipality permits for structural modifications, MEP changes or external alterations. We prepare all required drawings and documentation, submit on your behalf, and manage the complete approval process. This is included in our scope of work at no additional charge. We have established relationships with approval authorities across all major Dubai communities.

What areas in Dubai do you cover?

We cover all villa communities and apartment areas across Dubai including Palm Jumeirah, Emirates Hills, Arabian Ranches, Dubai Hills Estate, Jumeirah Golf Estates, Al Barari, DAMAC Hills, Springs and Meadows, Jumeirah Village Circle, Mirdif, Al Barsha, Downtown Dubai, Business Bay, Dubai Marina, JBR and all other residential areas. We have completed projects in 46+ communities across Dubai and are familiar with the specific NOC requirements, style expectations and typical layouts of each.

What is the difference between a renovation company and a fit-out company?

A renovation company upgrades or transforms existing finished spaces — replacing kitchens, bathrooms, flooring, and finishes in a property that is already lived in. A fit-out company takes an empty shell and core property and installs all finishes, MEP systems, and fixtures to make it move-in ready. First Unicorn Interiors does both — we are a combined renovation and fit-out company with in-house capability for every trade required in either scenario. One company for both services means less coordination overhead and better results.

Do you use subcontractors or is your team in-house?

Our core trades — electricians, plumbers, HVAC technicians, tilers, joiners, painters and stone installers — are all employed directly. This is the single biggest differentiator between us and most renovation companies in Dubai. In-house teams mean consistent quality, seamless coordination between trades, and the ability to resolve issues on site within hours rather than days. We do not subcontract the critical work. For specialist items such as custom metalwork or home automation, we work with vetted specialist partners under our supervision.
