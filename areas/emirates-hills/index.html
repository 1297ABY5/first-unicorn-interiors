What Makes Emirates Hills Unique

Emirates Hills is, quite simply, the most prestigious residential address in Dubai. Often referred to as the Beverly Hills of the Middle East, this gated community around the Montgomerie Golf Course demands a standard of renovation work that matches the expectations of its discerning residents. Villas here are not just homes — they are statements. Plot sizes range from 15,000 to over 100,000 square feet, and the architectural styles span classical Mediterranean, contemporary minimalist, and bespoke modern designs. Renovating in Emirates Hills means understanding that every detail, from the imported marble in the entrance hall to the bespoke joinery in the dressing rooms, must be executed to the highest possible standard.

Emaar, as the master developer, maintains strict community guidelines governing external modifications, boundary treatments, landscaping and any structural changes. All renovation work requires prior approval, and the submission process is thorough. Our team manages the complete approval cycle, preparing architectural drawings, engineering documentation and material specifications that meet Emaar's requirements. We have navigated this process across 23 Emirates Hills projects, and we understand exactly what the community management team expects to see in every submission.

The scale of Emirates Hills villas also presents unique project management challenges. These are large, complex properties with integrated landscaping, multiple outdoor living zones, expansive pools, and sophisticated mechanical and electrical systems. A full villa renovation in Emirates Hills is not simply a cosmetic refresh — it often involves upgrading HVAC systems, reconfiguring electrical distribution boards, installing home automation, and integrating indoor and outdoor spaces seamlessly. We bring a team of specialist subcontractors who have worked alongside us in this community for years, ensuring that the quality of workmanship matches the calibre of the address.
