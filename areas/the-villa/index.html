What Makes The Villa Unique

The Villa is a spacious community developed by Dubai Properties, located in Dubailand along the Hessa Street corridor. Completed around 2010, it features a variety of Spanish-influenced villa types — Centro, Palestra, Aldea, Ponderosa, Hacienda, Estella and Cordoba — offering some of the largest residential plots available in Dubai. With many homes now over fifteen years old, The Villa has entered its peak renovation cycle, and Our team has completed multiple projects across the community's diverse villa types.

What sets The Villa apart from other communities is the sheer size of the properties. The larger Hacienda and Cordoba villas can exceed 7,000 sqft of built-up area with plots over 10,000 sqft, making them ideal for families who want space and privacy. The original fitout across most villas was kept simple and functional, which means there is significant scope to personalise these generous spaces to a premium standard. This combination of large floor plans and straightforward starting finishes creates outstanding renovation potential. We regularly transform these vast villas into truly premium family homes with high-end kitchens, spa-quality bathrooms, cinema rooms and designer living spaces.

Pool construction and landscaping are particularly popular in The Villa due to the generous outdoor plots. Many homeowners invest in complete outdoor living packages including pool, covered majlis, outdoor kitchen, children's play area and professional landscaping. The community management approval process is straightforward compared to some Emaar or Nakheel communities, though engineering drawings are still required for any structural modifications or pool construction.

Common Villa Issues in The Villa

After working on 9+ projects in The Villa, we know exactly what to expect. Here are the issues we address most frequently:

  • Original fitout is simple — renovation budget recommended to unlock the full potential of these spacious homes
  • Basic tiling and kitchen specifications need complete replacement
  • Vast spaces that feel empty without proper interior design
  • Waterproofing issues common in ground-floor wet areas
  • Limited landscaping in most plots — bare sand or minimal planting

Every The Villa project begins with a thorough assessment. We identify hidden issues before they become expensive surprises during renovation.
