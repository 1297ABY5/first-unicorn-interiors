The Community

What Makes Dubai Islands Unique

Formerly Deira Islands, rebranded and reimagined as Dubai Islands — a cluster of five interconnected islands off the Deira coastline. Offering waterfront living with city views, beach access, and a hospitality-driven lifestyle. Positioned between Palm Jumeirah luxury and mainland affordability.

Villas range from 4br to 6br villas and beach houses, built between 2025–2028 handovers, new development. Property listings in Dubai Islands currently number 177+, making it one of Dubai's most active villa markets.

Renovation Intelligence

Common Villa Issues in Dubai Islands

Based on our experience working in this community, here are the renovation needs we see most often:

Post-handover customisation

New builds with developer-standard finishes. Buyers in the AED 5-15M range expect significant post-handover personalisation of kitchens, bathrooms, and living spaces.

Coastal material requirements

Sea-facing properties need marine-grade materials throughout — from aluminium systems to bathroom fixtures that resist salt air corrosion.

Outdoor entertainment spaces

Rooftop terraces, private pools, and outdoor dining areas need professional design and build to maximise waterfront views.

Lighting and view optimisation

Large glass facades and water views require automated blinds, UV film, and lighting design that complements the natural light patterns.
