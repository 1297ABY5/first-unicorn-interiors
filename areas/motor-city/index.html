What Makes Motor City Unique

Motor City and its adjoining Green Community offer a relaxed, family-friendly environment with a mix of townhouses and villas set around landscaped gardens and community parks. With 15 completed projects in the area, we understand the specific villa types, their common issues and the renovation approaches that deliver the best results. The Green Community villas, in particular, are well-suited to modernisation — solidly built with good bones but now showing their age in finishes and systems.

The most popular renovations in Motor City focus on practical family upgrades: kitchen modernisation with open-plan layouts, bathroom refreshes to replace worn fixtures and tiles, and flooring upgrades that transform the entire feel of the ground floor. The original finishes in Green Community villas were good quality for their time but now look dated compared to what buyers and tenants expect. We also frequently address outdoor spaces — adding pergolas, BBQ areas and garden landscaping that extend the useable living area.

Union Properties manages community approvals for Green Community, while Motor City falls under its own management structure. Either way, external modifications require proper NOC approval, and we handle this process for every project. The community's proximity to major roads and Autodrome makes it popular with professionals and families who value space without paying premium community prices.
