# SEO Playbook — Universal
## Version: 1.0 | Last Updated: 2026-02-20
## Keywords, locations, and topics come from /businesses/{name}/ config files.

## Keyword Strategy
- **Primary keywords:** Defined in business services.md + locations.md (e.g., "{service} {city}")
- **Secondary keywords:** Research-phase queries ("how much does {service} cost in {city}")
- **Long-tail:** Educational queries ("how to choose {provider} in {city}")
- Agent generates keyword matrix by combining services × locations × intents

## Blog Strategy
- **Frequency:** Defined in business targets.md blog_frequency
- **Length:** 600-800 words minimum
- **Structure:** H1 → intro → H2 sections → conclusion → CTA
- **Internal links:** Always link to relevant service page + 1-2 other posts
- **Meta description:** Under 155 chars, include primary keyword
- **Images:** 2-3 per post (real photos preferred, AI for illustration)

## Blog Title Formats (Universal Templates)
1. "How We {Achieved Result} in {Location} — {Specific Detail}"
2. "The Real Cost of {Service} in {City} — {Year} Breakdown"
3. "{Number} Things Every {Audience} Should Know About {Topic}"
4. "{Option A} vs {Option B}: An Honest Comparison from {Years} of Experience"
5. "What Your {Provider}'s {Aspect} Tells You About Them"
6. "{Number} Mistakes to Avoid When {Action}"

## Local SEO (for businesses with physical presence)
- Google Business Profile: update weekly with posts + photos
- NAP consistency across all relevant directories
- Reviews: request from every completed customer (see conversion playbook)
- Respond to every review within 24 hours
- Directory listings: from business locations.md local_directories

## Technical SEO (Universal)
- Page speed: <3 seconds load
- Mobile-first (assume 80%+ mobile traffic)
- Schema markup: appropriate type from schema.org
- XML sitemap submitted to Search Console
- Canonical URLs on all pages
- Structured data for FAQ, Reviews, LocalBusiness where applicable

## Content-to-Page Strategy
- Create a landing page for each: service × top location combination
- URL pattern: /{service}-{location}
- Each page has unique H1, content, images, CTA

## PROVEN Patterns
_Cross-business patterns only. Per-business in /businesses/{name}/results/_

## AVOID Patterns
_Cross-business avoids only._
