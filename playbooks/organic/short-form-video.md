## Format Rules
- Hook in first 2 seconds (text on screen + strongest visual)
- Fast cuts between key moments
- End with: logo + primary contact + CTA text
- Under 60 seconds
- Vertical only (9:16)
- Captions/subtitles always on (80% watch muted)
- Trending audio when appropriate, clean ambient otherwise
