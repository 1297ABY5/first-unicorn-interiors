## Carousel Rules (Universal)
- Slide 1: Hook (bold statement or question ‚Äî NEVER start with company name)
- Slide 2: Context (before state, pain point, problem, or surprising fact)
- Slides 3-5: Details (one element per slide, include specifics from business services.md)
- Slide 6: Reveal/Result (emotional language about the outcome)
- Slide 7: CTA (from business brand.md)

## Reel Structure (Universal)
- 0-3s: Hook (text overlay ‚Äî strongest visual or provocative claim)
- 3-8s: Before/context with voiceover
- 8-20s: Transformation/process sequence
- 20-28s: Reveal ‚Äî slow reveal of finished result
- 28-30s: CTA text overlay + logo from business brand.md
- Voiceover: First person, business voice, under 80 words
- Text overlays: Max 5 words per overlay

## Story Rules (Universal)
- Use polls, sliders, question stickers for engagement
- Behind-the-scenes and process content performs best
- Always include primary CTA link on final story
- Post 3-7 stories per sequence

## Image Guidelines (Universal)
- Real work photos: ALWAYS for results, testimonials, team, proof
- AI-generated: Inspiration, lifestyle, mood boards, aspirational only
- Branded graphics: Carousels with text, stat posts, listing graphics
- NEVER pass AI as actual work
