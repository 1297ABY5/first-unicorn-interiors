# Image Generation Playbook — Universal
## Version: 1.0 | Last Updated: 2026-02-20

## Tools
| Tool | Cost | Use Case |
|------|------|----------|
| Flux via Replicate | ~$0.03/img | Photorealistic AI images |
| Puppeteer | FREE | HTML → PNG branded graphics |
| Sharp (npm) | FREE | Resize, crop, watermark |

## GOLDEN RULE: AI for aspiration. Real photos for proof. NEVER fake actual work.

## When to Use AI Images
✅ Blog post hero images
✅ Landing page lifestyle/aspirational shots
✅ Inspiration posts (clearly aspirational)
✅ Ad backgrounds and lifestyle imagery
✅ Concept renders for planned work
✅ Material/texture close-ups for education
✅ Generic industry imagery

## When REAL Photos are Mandatory
❌ Results of actual completed work (before/after)
❌ Team and people photos
❌ Client testimonials
❌ Google Business Profile
❌ Anything claiming "we did this" or "our work"
❌ Progress/construction/process shots

## Flux Prompt Template Structure
"Professional {photography_type} of a {adjective} {subject} in {location_style}, {detail_1}, {detail_2}, {lighting}, {quality_descriptor}"

## Puppeteer Templates (branded graphics)
- Load brand colours, fonts, logo from /businesses/{name}/brand.md
- Carousel slides: clean background + text + image area + logo
- Before/after split: side-by-side with divider + labels + logo
- Stat cards: large number + context + brand colours
- Testimonial cards: quote + stars + name + brand frame
- Listing graphics: key details + image + price + CTA
