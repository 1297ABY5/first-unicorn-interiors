## Video Types (ranked)
1. Before/after transformation (15-30s)
2. Process/day-in-the-life footage (30-60s)
3. Comparison (A vs B) (15-30s)
4. Timelapse of work (15-30s)
5. Client testimonial (30-60s)
6. Quick tip/education (15-30s)

## Structure (all short-form)
- 0-3s: Hook (text overlay + strongest visual)
- 3-25s: Body (story, transformation, education)
- 25-28s: Reveal (finished result)
- 28-30s: CTA (logo + contact from brand.md)

## Rules
- Captions/subtitles always (80% watch muted)
- Vertical for social, horizontal for website/YouTube
- End card: logo + primary contact + CTA from brand.md
- Music: trending or royalty-free ambient
