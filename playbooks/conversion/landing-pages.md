# Landing Pages Playbook — Universal
## Version: 1.0 | Last Updated: 2026-02-20

## Page Types
1. **Service page** — one per service from services.md
2. **Location page** — one per service × tier-1 location combination
3. **Offer page** — seasonal, promotional, or campaign-specific

## Universal Structure
1. Hero: H1 headline + subheadline + primary CTA + hero image
2. Trust bar: top 4 trust signals from brand.md
3. Portfolio/proof gallery (3-5 examples)
4. Service breakdown (what's included, from services.md)
5. Process steps (from brand.md process_steps, typically 3-5)
6. Testimonials (2-3 reviews)
7. FAQ (3-5 questions from audiences.md pain points)
8. Final CTA: primary + secondary CTAs from brand.md

## Mobile Optimisation (non-negotiable)
- Primary CTA sticky on mobile
- Load <3 seconds
- Form: max 4 fields
- Tap-to-call/chat enabled
- Images lazy-loaded

## A/B Testing
- Test hero headline (2 variants minimum)
- Test CTA button text
- Test with/without pricing
- Test form length (3 vs 4 fields)
- Test social proof positioning (above vs below fold)
