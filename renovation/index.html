Renovation Division

Every Room. Every Detail.
One Accountable Team.

Most renovation companies in Dubai are brokers. They take your deposit, subcontract every trade, and disappear when problems surface. We do things differently.

First Unicorn Interiors employs its own carpenters, tilers, plumbers, electricians and painters. Our workshop fabricates custom joinery in-house. Our waterproofing specialists guarantee every wet area. And one project manager oversees your build from day one to handover.

The result: tighter timelines, consistent quality, and a single point of accountability. No finger-pointing between subcontractors. No surprise variation orders. Just honest renovation, delivered properly.
