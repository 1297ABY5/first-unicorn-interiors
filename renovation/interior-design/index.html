The Problem

Why Interior Design Projects Fail in Dubai

Most interior design companies in Dubai create beautiful renders but outsource the execution. The design team draws it, then a separate contractor builds it — and the result never matches the concept. Colours shift, materials get substituted, and the design intent is lost in translation.

We do it differently. Our interior designers and renovation team work under one roof. The person who designs your space supervises the build. What you see in 3D is what you get on handover day.

  • Design and build under one company — no outsourcing
  • Photorealistic 3D visualisation before any work starts
  • Direct material sourcing from European and local suppliers
  • Fixed-price execution — no variation orders mid-project
