The Problem

Common Pool Problems in Dubai

Dubai's extreme heat, high salinity and intense UV exposure take a brutal toll on swimming pools. Tiles crack and delaminate. Grout deteriorates. Filtration systems struggle to keep up with sand and dust. Decking fades, cracks and becomes dangerously hot underfoot.

Many pool renovation and installation companies in Dubai focus only on one element — they retile but leave the outdated filtration. They resurface the deck but ignore the failing pump. The pool looks better for a few months, then the underlying problems resurface.

  • Cracked and delaminating tiles from thermal expansion
  • Failing filtration systems that cannot cope with sand and dust
  • Outdated decking that is dangerously hot, faded and cracked
  • Poor lighting and safety features that do not meet current standards

Pool Types We Build

Every Style. Every Size. Built for Dubai.

From budget-friendly skimmer pools to ultra-luxury knife-edge designs — we engineer, approve and build every pool type for Dubai's climate and your villa's layout.

Budget-Friendly

Skimmer Pools

The reliable, proven option for Dubai villas. Water is pulled through surface skimmers into the filtration system, keeping construction costs lower while delivering excellent results. The most popular choice for family homes across Arabian Ranches, Dubai Hills and Jumeirah Park.

Compact

Plunge Pools

Smaller, deeper pools built for cooling off rather than swimming lengths — perfect for urban homes, townhouse gardens and smaller villa plots. Typically 3–5 metres across and 1.5–2 metres deep. In Dubai's 50-degree summers, a plunge pool is the fastest way to cool down.

Premium

Overflow Pools

Water spills over into hidden perimeter channels, maintaining a constant level and creating a seamless, mirror-like surface. More complex than skimmer systems — requires a balance tank and higher-capacity pumps — but the visual impact is extraordinary. Popular in Emirates Hills and Meydan.

Wellness

Jacuzzi & Spa Pools

Luxury wellness additions featuring heated water, therapeutic jets and integrated seating — built for relaxation after a long day. We install standalone spa pools, raised jacuzzi units adjacent to the main pool, and integrated spa zones within larger pool designs. Heated, jet-driven and built for year-round use.

Signature Dubai

Infinity Pools

The signature Dubai pool. One or more edges vanish into a catch basin, creating the illusion of water merging with the horizon. Requires precise engineering — the weir edge must be levelled to within 1mm tolerance. Stunning when designed to frame a golf course, skyline or garden view.

Eco-Friendly

Natural Pools

Eco-conscious pools that use biological filtration with plants, gravel and natural stone instead of harsh chemicals. A regeneration zone filters the water naturally, creating a swimming experience that blends seamlessly with the landscape. Ideal for Al Barari, Sustainable City and nature-focused homeowners.

Ultra-Luxury

Knife-Edge Pools

The pinnacle of modern pool design. The water edge is perfectly flush with the surrounding deck, creating a razor-sharp, mirror-like surface with invisible drainage. Every millimetre matters — meticulous craftsmanship required. Reserved for clients who accept nothing less than architectural perfection.

Elevated

Rooftop & Terrace Pools

Custom-engineered lightweight pools designed for installation on rooftops, elevated terraces and upper-level decks. Structural load calculations, waterproofing membrane systems and specialised drainage are critical. We work with structural engineers to ensure your building can support the weight safely.

Fast & Durable

Fibreglass Pools

Pre-moulded pool shells that are quick to install, highly durable, low-maintenance and smooth to the touch. A fibreglass pool can be installed in as little as two weeks — compared to six to eight weeks for concrete. Excellent for clients who want speed without sacrificing quality or longevity.

Luxury Statement

Glass-Walled & Feature Pools

Built to impress. These pools use toughened acrylic or glass panels for one or more sides, creating a dramatic see-through effect. Often integrated with fire bowls, waterfalls, rain curtains or ambient LED lighting. Our glass and aluminium specialists fabricate and install the panels in-house.

Fitness

Lap Pools

Long, narrow and purpose-built for swimming workouts. Typically 12–25 metres long and 2.5–3.5 metres wide. The slim footprint fits side yards, boundary walls or as a dramatic linear feature. We install counter-current jet systems for pools under 12 metres, allowing endless swimming in compact spaces.

A pool tile replacement and resurfacing typically takes 3-4 weeks. A complete pool renovation including deck, tiling, filtration and lighting requires 6-8 weeks. New pool construction takes 8-12 weeks depending on size and complexity. We schedule pool work during cooler months when possible, as extreme summer heat affects curing times for certain materials.
We recommend glass mosaic tiles or porcelain pool tiles rated for full immersion. Glass mosaics are the most popular choice for Dubai pools — they are non-porous, chemical-resistant, UV-stable and available in a wide range of colours. For the pool surround, we use anti-slip porcelain or natural stone with appropriate slip ratings. We avoid materials that deteriorate under Dubai's intense UV exposure and chlorinated water.

Modern systems reduce chemical usage, energy costs and maintenance requirements significantly.
