The Problem

Why Duplex Renovations Need Coordinated Teams

Duplexes require renovation work across two connected levels simultaneously. The staircase is both a structural element and a design centrepiece. Flooring transitions between levels must be seamless. MEP systems span both floors. Most contractors treat each level separately, creating disconnected results.

We renovate duplexes as one integrated project. Our team works on both levels with a unified design concept, coordinated material flow, and a single project timeline. The result is a cohesive home, not two separate floors.

  • Both levels renovated as one integrated project
  • Staircase design — the centrepiece of every duplex
  • Seamless flooring transitions between levels
  • Coordinated MEP across both floors
