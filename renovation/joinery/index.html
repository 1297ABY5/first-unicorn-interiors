The Problem

Why Off-the-Shelf Joinery Fails in Dubai

Dubai villas are not built to standard European dimensions. Ceiling heights vary from 3.2 to 4.5 metres. Bedroom layouts are irregular. Alcoves, columns and services runs create awkward spaces that no flat-pack wardrobe will ever fill properly. The result is wasted space — dead zones above wardrobes, gaps between units and walls, and storage that does not suit how you actually live.

Then there is the climate problem. Imported joinery — particularly solid timber pieces shipped from Europe or the Far East — is manufactured for a completely different humidity environment. Within months of installation in a Dubai villa, doors start to warp, drawers stick, veneers lift and joints crack. The wood was never acclimatised to the UAE, and the finishes were never formulated for 80%+ humidity levels. We see this constantly when renovating homes with joinery that is only two or three years old.

  • Standard furniture dimensions waste valuable space in Dubai villas
  • Imported timber warps and cracks in Dubai's high humidity
  • Budget contractors use thin 12mm MDF that sags under weight
  • Poor finishing — uneven lacquer, visible joins, misaligned doors

Outdoor Joinery

Garden storage, poolside cabinetry and outdoor kitchen units built with marine-grade plywood, stainless steel hardware and UV-resistant finishes. Engineered to withstand Dubai's extreme summer heat and humidity without warping, fading or corroding.

Our Process

From Concept to Installation

Survey & Design

Precision laser measurement of your spaces, followed by a full 3D design with material selections, colour samples and a detailed quotation. You approve everything before fabrication begins.

2

Material Selection

Choose from our range of substrates, veneers, lacquer colours, laminates and hardware. We provide physical samples so you can see and touch every material that will be used in your joinery.

Fabrication

Your joinery is built in our own workshop by our in-house carpenters using CNC precision cutting, edge banding and spray finishing. Quality checked at every stage before leaving the workshop.

Installation & Handover

On-site installation by the same team that built your joinery. Final adjustments, hardware alignment, touch-ups and a thorough clean. Handover with warranty certificate.
