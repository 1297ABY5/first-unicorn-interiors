The Problem

Why Precision Matters in Glass Works

Glass is unforgiving. A 2mm error in measurement means the panel does not fit. A poorly aligned hinge causes the door to swing incorrectly. An improperly sealed shower enclosure leaks water across your bathroom floor. There is no margin for error and no way to patch mistakes without starting over.

Many glass companies in Dubai outsource fabrication and installation to different teams. The person who measures is not the person who cuts, and neither is the person who installs. Dimensions get lost in translation. Hardware arrives wrong. The result is re-work, delays and compromised quality.

  • Measurement errors that require costly re-fabrication
  • Poor waterproofing at glass-to-tile junctions in showers
  • Misaligned hardware that affects door swing and closure
  • Safety standards ignored — non-tempered glass in wet areas
What We Do

Glass & Aluminium Services

Frameless Glass

Shower enclosures, partitions, doors and feature walls in frameless tempered glass. Minimal hardware, maximum visual impact. Clean lines with precision engineering.

Tempered Safety

All our glass is tempered to UAE safety standards. 10mm and 12mm options for different applications. If broken, it shatters safely into small granular pieces rather than dangerous shards.

Shower Enclosures

Walk-in showers, hinged doors, sliding systems and curved enclosures. Every installation is waterproofed at the glass-to-tile junction with medical-grade silicone for a permanent seal.

Balustrades

Frameless and semi-frameless glass balustrades for balconies, terraces, staircases and pool areas. Base channel, standoff and point-fixed systems. All compliant with Dubai building codes.

Facades

Aluminium curtain wall systems, storefront glazing and window replacement. European-profile aluminium with high-performance double-glazed units for superior thermal insulation.

Precision Installation

One team measures, fabricates and installs. Rajeev and his specialist glazing crew have been with us for over a decade. They understand tolerances, they coordinate with tilers, and they get it right first time.

Common Questions

Glass & Aluminium FAQ

We use 10mm or 12mm tempered safety glass for all shower enclosures. Tempered glass is up to five times stronger than standard glass and, if broken, shatters into small harmless pieces rather than dangerous shards. We offer clear, ultra-clear (low iron) and frosted options. All glass is certified to UAE safety standards and comes with polished or bevelled edges for a premium finish.
From site measurement to installation, a frameless shower enclosure typically takes 7-10 working days. The process involves a precision site survey, glass fabrication to exact dimensions, hardware procurement and on-site installation. Installation itself takes 1-2 days depending on complexity. We coordinate with tiling and waterproofing teams if the shower is part of a larger bathroom renovation.
Yes. We design and install frameless and semi-frameless glass balustrades for balconies, terraces, staircases and mezzanines. All installations use tempered or laminated safety glass and comply with Dubai building codes for height, loading and fixing requirements. We work with base channel, standoff and point-fixed systems depending on the design and structural requirements of your project.
Yes. We supply and install aluminium windows, sliding doors, folding doors and curtain wall systems. We work with premium European profiles that offer excellent thermal performance — critical in Dubai's climate for keeping energy costs down. All systems are powder-coated in your choice of colour and fitted with high-performance double-glazed units for sound insulation and UV protection.
Framed glass uses visible aluminium profiles around every edge and is the most economical option. Semi-frameless uses a frame on some edges (typically the top and sides) with a clean bottom edge. Frameless uses minimal hardware — typically just hinges and a handle — with no visible frame, creating a sleek, modern aesthetic. We specialise in frameless installations which require precision engineering and expert fitting to ensure perfect alignment and waterproofing.
