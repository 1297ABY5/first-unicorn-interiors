The Problem

Why Penthouse Renovations Demand Expertise

Penthouses are not large apartments. They are unique properties with double-height ceilings, terrace access, custom HVAC requirements, weight load considerations for heavy materials, and complex logistics for material delivery to high floors. Standard apartment contractors are out of their depth.

We specialise in high-end penthouse renovations across Dubai's premium towers. We understand the engineering, the logistics, and the design language that penthouse living demands.

  • Structural load assessment for marble, stone, and water features
  • High-floor logistics — material delivery and crane coordination
  • Double-height ceiling design and lighting expertise
  • Building management coordination for premium towers
