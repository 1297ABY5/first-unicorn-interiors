The Problem

Why Dubai Villas Need Solar

Dubai receives over 350 days of sunshine per year, yet most villa owners are paying full DEWA rates for electricity and water heating. With average villa DEWA bills ranging from AED 2,000 to AED 8,000 per month, the opportunity to reduce costs through solar is significant — and the technology has never been more accessible or affordable.

Many homeowners are put off by confusion around DEWA's Shams initiative, concerns about roof suitability, or uncertainty about return on investment. Others have received quotes from solar-only companies that lack construction expertise — leading to poor installations, roof leaks and systems that underperform. As a licensed construction and renovation company, we bring structural knowledge to every solar installation, ensuring your roof, waterproofing and electrical systems are handled properly.

  • Average Dubai villa pays AED 3,000-8,000/month in DEWA bills
  • Solar panels can reduce electricity costs by 30-40%
  • Solar water heaters eliminate gas or electric heating costs entirely
  • DEWA Shams programme allows net metering — sell excess power back to the grid
What We Do

Complete Solar Services

Solar Panel Systems

Rooftop photovoltaic systems sized for your villa's consumption. Tier-1 panels, micro-inverters, monitoring systems and full DEWA Shams registration. Typical systems: 5kW to 20kW for residential villas.

Solar Water Heaters

Evacuated tube and flat plate solar thermal systems that heat your villa's water using free sunlight. Eliminates electric or gas water heating costs year-round. Ideal for Dubai's climate with ROI typically under 2 years.

DEWA Shams Registration

We handle the complete DEWA Shams application — from initial feasibility assessment to final connection approval. Net metering allows you to export excess electricity back to the grid, earning credits on your DEWA bill.

Structural Assessment

Unlike solar-only companies, we are a licensed construction firm. We assess your roof's structural capacity, waterproofing integrity and electrical infrastructure before installation — preventing the leaks and damage that plague poorly installed systems.

Solar Pool Heating

Extend your swimming season year-round with solar pool heating systems. Unglazed collector panels heat pool water efficiently using Dubai's abundant sunshine — no running costs after installation.

Maintenance & Monitoring

Annual maintenance packages including panel cleaning, inverter checks, performance monitoring and system optimisation. Remote monitoring alerts you to any underperformance before it affects your savings.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

A typical residential solar panel system costs AED 25,000 to AED 80,000 depending on size. A 10kW system suitable for a 4-5 bedroom villa typically costs AED 40,000-55,000 installed. Solar water heaters start from AED 8,000-15,000. ROI is typically 3-5 years for panels and 1-2 years for water heaters.

DEWA Shams is Dubai's net metering programme allowing villa owners to install solar panels connected to the grid. Excess electricity is exported and credited to your DEWA account. You only pay for net consumption. We handle the complete Shams application.

Physical installation takes 2-3 days for a standard system. The complete process including structural assessment, DEWA application, approval and connection takes 4-8 weeks. We manage the entire timeline.
