The Problem

Why Full Villa Renovations Fail in Dubai

Most villa renovations in Dubai are managed by companies that subcontract every single trade. Your kitchen goes to one team, your bathrooms to another, your flooring to a third, and your joinery to a fourth. Nobody coordinates. Nobody takes ownership.

The result is predictable: misaligned timelines, clashing finishes, hidden variation orders and months of delays. The contractor blames the subcontractor, the subcontractor blames the supplier, and you are left standing in a half-finished house wondering where your money went.

  • Multiple subcontractors with no single point of accountability
  • Poor coordination between trades causes rework and delays
  • Hidden costs surface mid-project as "variation orders"
  • No design continuity — each team interprets the brief differently

Common Questions

Full Villa Renovation FAQ

A complete villa renovation typically takes 8 to 16 weeks depending on the size of the villa, the scope of work and the complexity of custom elements. A standard 3-bedroom villa refresh can be completed in 8-10 weeks, while a luxury 5-bedroom transformation with custom joinery and pool work may require 14-16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline at quotation stage and update you weekly on progress against schedule.
For partial renovations — a single kitchen or bathroom, for example — it is sometimes possible to remain in the property with some disruption. For full villa renovations involving demolition, MEP works and flooring, we strongly recommend relocating for the duration. Dust, noise and safety concerns make it impractical and uncomfortable to live on site during major works. Most of our clients move to a short-term rental or stay with family during the build.
Our turnkey package includes demolition and disposal, structural modifications (if required), all MEP works (mechanical, electrical, plumbing and AC), waterproofing in wet areas, flooring throughout, wall finishes, custom joinery (wardrobes, kitchen, vanities), bathroom renovation, painting, lighting installation and final deep cleaning. We handle everything from foundation to finishing touches — you do not need to coordinate separate contractors.
Yes. We manage all permit applications, NOCs from community management bodies (such as Nakheel for Palm Jumeirah, Emaar for Arabian Ranches and Dubai Hills, or DHAM for various developments), and any municipality approvals required. This is included as part of our project management service at no additional cost. We have established relationships with all major community management offices, which helps expedite the process.
We operate on a milestone-based payment structure tied to completed work. A typical schedule is: 30% upon contract signing and material procurement, 25% at rough-in completion (MEP and structural works), 25% at finishing stage (tiling, joinery installation, painting), and 20% upon handover and final inspection. This structure protects both parties — you only pay for completed work, and we maintain the cash flow needed to keep the project moving on schedule.
Every full villa renovation comes with a 3-year craftsmanship warranty covering all workmanship performed by our team. This includes tiling, joinery, painting, plumbing fixtures and electrical installations. If anything we installed fails due to workmanship within that period, we return and repair it at no cost. Manufacturer warranties on appliances, fixtures and materials are passed through separately and typically range from 2 to 10 years depending on the product.
