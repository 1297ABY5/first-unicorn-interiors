The Problem

Why Apartment Renovations Go Wrong in Dubai

Apartment renovations in Dubai come with unique challenges: building management restrictions, neighbour noise limits, limited working hours, elevator booking requirements, and material delivery constraints. Most general contractors treat apartments like villas and run into problems from day one.

We specialise in apartment renovations across Dubai Marina, Downtown, JBR, Business Bay, and every major tower community. We know the building rules, we handle the NOCs, and we work within the permitted hours to keep your building management happy.

  • Building management NOCs and permits handled by us
  • Work within permitted hours — no complaints from neighbours
  • Material delivery coordinated with building security
  • Dust and noise containment for occupied buildings
