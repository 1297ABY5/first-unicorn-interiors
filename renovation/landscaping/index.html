The Problem

The Outdoor Living Opportunity

Dubai villa owners spend millions on interior renovations but often neglect the space with the most potential — the garden. Yet with 300+ days of usable outdoor weather and plot sizes ranging from 3,000 to 30,000 square feet, the garden represents the single largest untapped living space in most villas. A well-designed outdoor area does not just look good — it fundamentally changes how you use your home.

The challenge is that Dubai's climate demands specialist knowledge. Plants that thrive in temperate gardens die within weeks in 50-degree heat. Irrigation systems need to balance water conservation with plant survival. Hardscaping materials must resist thermal expansion, UV degradation and salt exposure. And outdoor structures — pergolas, kitchens, seating areas — need to be engineered for occasional but extreme wind loads during shamal seasons.

  • Most villa gardens are underutilised — generic developer landscaping or bare sand
  • Wrong plant selection leads to constant replacement and wasted water
  • Poor irrigation design wastes water and fails to keep planting alive
  • Outdoor structures built without engineering fail in extreme weather
What We Do

Complete Outdoor Services

Garden Design

Complete landscape architecture — from concept sketches to 3D visualisation and planting plans. We design gardens that look stunning year-round using drought-tolerant species suited to Dubai's climate, soil conditions and water restrictions.

Outdoor Kitchens & BBQ

Custom-built outdoor cooking and entertaining areas with built-in grills, pizza ovens, countertops, refrigeration and seating. Designed to withstand Dubai summers while creating a resort-like entertaining experience.

Pergolas & Shade Structures

Aluminium and timber pergolas, motorised louvre systems, retractable awnings and shade sails. Engineered for Dubai's wind loads and UV exposure. Create usable outdoor space even in peak summer months.

Hardscaping & Paving

Driveways, pathways, patios, retaining walls and decorative stonework. Natural stone, porcelain pavers, stamped concrete and interlocking blocks — all selected for Dubai's thermal and UV conditions.

Irrigation Systems

Smart drip irrigation, automated controllers, rain sensors and zone-based watering systems. Designed to minimise water waste while keeping your garden thriving. Compliant with Dubai Municipality water conservation requirements.

Garden Lighting

Landscape uplighting, pathway lights, feature illumination and automated control systems. Low-voltage LED systems that create dramatic evening ambiance while minimising energy consumption. Transform your garden after dark.

Frequently Asked Questions

Basic landscaping starts from AED 30,000-50,000. Comprehensive outdoor living projects with pergola, outdoor kitchen, premium planting and lighting range from AED 80,000-250,000. Large estates can exceed AED 500,000.

We use drought-tolerant species: bougainvillea, jasmine, frangipani, date palms, ghaf trees, desert roses, oleander and succulents. Designed to look lush year-round with minimal water.
