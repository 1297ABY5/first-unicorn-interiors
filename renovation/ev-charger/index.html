Why Home Charging

Stop Queuing at Public Chargers

Public charging in Dubai costs AED 0.70–1.25 per kWh. Home charging costs AED 0.30–0.44 per kWh. That is 40–60% cheaper per charge — and you never queue, never detour, never wait.

A typical EV driven 40 km per day costs roughly AED 150–200 per month to charge at home versus AED 350–500 at public stations. Over a year, home charging saves AED 2,000–3,600. The charger pays for itself.

Villa owners have the easiest setup in Dubai — direct wiring from your electrical panel to your garage or driveway. No building management approval, no shared infrastructure complications. Plug in when you get home, wake up to a full battery.
