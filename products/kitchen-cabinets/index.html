Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full kitchen take?
4-5 weeks from design approval to completion. Cabinets are built in 2-3 weeks, installed in 2-3 days, countertop fitted within the following week.
Can you work around existing plumbing and electrics?
Yes. We design around your existing services. If changes are needed, our team handles plumbing and electrical modifications.
What is kitchen refacing?
Refacing means keeping your existing cabinet boxes and replacing just the doors, drawer fronts, and handles. It gives you a brand new look at 40-50% of the cost of a full kitchen. Takes 2-3 days.
Do you supply appliances?
We can source and install appliances, or design around appliances you've already purchased. We work with all brands and ensure proper cutouts and ventilation.
