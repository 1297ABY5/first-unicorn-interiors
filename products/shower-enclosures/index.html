Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?
From measurement to installation, 7-10 working days. The actual fitting takes 3-4 hours.
What thickness of glass do you use?
10mm tempered safety glass as standard. 12mm available for larger panels. All glass is certified to UAE safety standards.
Do you remove the old shower enclosure?
Yes, removal of existing enclosure is included in the price. We dispose of old glass safely.
Can you install on tiled walls?
Yes. We drill through tiles using diamond core bits to prevent cracking. All fixings are sealed to prevent water ingress.
What hardware finishes are available?
Chrome, brushed nickel, matt black, brushed gold, and satin brass. Hardware is marine-grade stainless steel.
Do you offer a warranty?
1-year warranty on installation and hardware. Glass itself carries a lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects.
