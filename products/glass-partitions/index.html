Frequently Asked Questions

Are glass partitions soundproof?
Standard glass partitions reduce noise by 30-35dB. Acoustic-rated options achieve up to 42dB — sufficient for meeting rooms and private offices.
Can you install partitions in an existing office?
Yes. Our systems require minimal structural work — just floor and ceiling tracks. No wet trades, no painting required.
Do you offer privacy options?
Yes — frosted, tinted, film-applied patterns, integrated blinds, and electrically switchable smart glass.
