What Makes City Walk & Al Wasl Unique

City Walk and the surrounding Al Wasl district represent a different kind of Dubai living — low-rise, walkable, design-forward and genuinely urban. With 15 completed projects in this Meraas-developed neighbourhood, we understand the premium expectations, building management standards and design sensibilities that City Walk residents demand. These are architecturally distinctive buildings that require renovation approaches matching their design intent.

City Walk apartments are relatively new, so renovation here focuses on personalisation rather than remediation. Homeowners typically seek to replace developer-standard kitchens with custom designs, upgrade bathrooms with premium natural stone, install bespoke joinery and integrate smart home technology. The apartments' generous proportions and contemporary architecture provide an excellent canvas for interior design that is both functional and visually striking.

Meraas maintains high community standards, and all renovation work must comply with specific guidelines regarding materials, noise levels and contractor qualifications. We are approved to work in Meraas communities and manage the approval process from initial application to completion sign-off. The district's reputation for design quality means that renovation work here must be executed to an exceptionally high standard — anything less is immediately noticeable.
