Questions
Do I need to sell or convince anyone? +
No. You just mention that you know a renovation company and share our WhatsApp number. We handle everything after that — the call, the site visit, the quote, the project. You don't need to sell.
When do I get paid? +
AED 500 is paid within 3 days of the client's site visit. The bonus (AED 500-1,000 more) is paid when the project starts. Cash or bank transfer — your choice.
How do you track my referrals? +
When you join, we give you a referral code. The client mentions your code when they message us, or you create a WhatsApp group with us and the client. Every referral is logged under your name. You can check your balance anytime.
Will this affect my main job? +
Not at all. You're not doing extra work — you're just mentioning us during your regular visits. It takes 30 seconds. Please ensure you comply with any obligations under your own employment contract.
What if the client doesn't proceed with renovation? +
You still get AED 500 for the site visit referral. The client gets a free quote. Nobody loses anything. Even if the client decides not to proceed right now, they often come back months later — and your referral is still on file.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer? +
No limit. Refer 1 person or 50. Every qualified referral earns you money. Our top referral partners send 5-10 leads per month.
Can I refer friends and family too? +
Yes. Anyone who needs renovation work in Dubai. Not limited to your work clients.
