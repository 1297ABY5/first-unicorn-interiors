The Challenge

How Your Space Affects Your Bottom Line

There is a direct, measurable link between the quality of a commercial environment and business performance. Research consistently shows that well-designed offices increase productivity by 15 to 20 percent. Retail spaces with considered layouts and lighting see higher dwell times and conversion rates. Restaurants where acoustics, lighting and seating have been properly engineered generate more covers per evening and stronger repeat custom.

Yet many businesses in Dubai treat their interior fit-out as an afterthought — something to get done quickly and cheaply before opening day. The result is a generic space that looks like every other office in Business Bay or every other restaurant on JBR. It does nothing to differentiate the brand, does nothing to attract talent, and actively undermines the customer experience.

The commercial fit-out landscape in Dubai also has regulatory complexity that catches many business owners off guard. Municipality approvals, civil defence requirements, DEWA coordination, building management restrictions on working hours, and trade licence-specific layout rules all need to be navigated before a single wall is moved. Engaging a fit-out company that understands these requirements from day one is the difference between opening on schedule and facing costly delays. Our team has delivered commercial projects across free zones, mainland Dubai and Abu Dhabi — we know the process inside out and handle all approvals as part of our scope.

What We Deliver

Commercial Design Services

Space Planning

We start with how your business operates. For offices, that means understanding team structures, meeting patterns and growth plans. For retail, it means customer flow, display hierarchies and point-of-sale positioning. For restaurants, it means kitchen workflow, service routes and covers per square metre. The layout drives everything that follows.

Brand Integration

Your space should feel unmistakably yours. We translate brand guidelines — colours, typography, values, personality — into physical environments. From reception feature walls and branded wayfinding to material choices that reflect your positioning, every element reinforces who you are to clients, customers and staff.

Compliance

Dubai Municipality, civil defence, DEWA, Trakhees, DDA accessibility requirements — commercial fit-outs involve multiple approval bodies. We prepare all drawings to compliance standards, submit on your behalf, and manage the approval process end to end. Our track record means fewer rejections and faster timelines.

Furniture & FF&E

We procure commercial-grade furniture, fixtures and equipment from manufacturers who understand the demands of high-traffic environments. Ergonomic office chairs, custom reception desks, restaurant banquette seating, retail display systems — all sourced, delivered and installed as part of the project.

Lighting Systems

Commercial lighting is not just about illumination — it is a tool for productivity, ambience and energy efficiency. We design lighting schemes that meet Dubai Green Building standards, incorporate smart controls where appropriate, and create the right atmosphere for each zone within your space.

Phased Delivery

For businesses that cannot shut down entirely during fit-out, we offer phased delivery. We work zone by zone, outside operating hours where needed, ensuring minimal disruption to your operations. Detailed scheduling and coordination with building management keep everything on track.

Common Questions

Commercial Interior Design FAQ

Do you handle Dubai Municipality approvals for commercial fit-outs?

Yes. Every commercial fit-out in Dubai requires municipality approval, and the requirements differ depending on whether the space is an office, restaurant, retail unit or medical facility. We handle the full approval process including floor plan submissions, civil defence compliance, DEWA coordination and trade licence-related approvals. This is included in our project scope.

Can you work outside business hours to avoid disruption?

Absolutely. For occupied buildings and operating businesses, we routinely schedule noisy works during evenings, weekends or building-permitted hours. We also offer phased delivery so parts of your space can remain operational while others are being fitted out. This is particularly common with retail and restaurant projects.

How long does a commercial interior fit-out take?

A small office fit-out typically takes 4 to 8 weeks. A medium-sized commercial space such as a restaurant or clinic takes 8 to 14 weeks. Large-format retail or multi-floor offices can take 4 to 6 months. Lead times depend on design complexity, material procurement and the approval process. We provide a detailed programme during the design phase.

Do you supply furniture and FF&E for commercial projects?

Yes. We handle complete FF&E (furniture, fixtures and equipment) procurement for commercial projects. This includes office workstations, reception desks, restaurant seating, display units, lighting systems and acoustic solutions. We source from commercial-grade manufacturers and can provide custom-built pieces where standard products do not meet the brief.

What types of commercial spaces do you design?

We design and fit out offices (from startup spaces to corporate headquarters), retail stores and showrooms, restaurants and cafes, medical clinics, salons and spas, co-working spaces, and hotel lobbies and common areas. Each type has specific regulatory requirements and design considerations that our team is experienced in navigating.
