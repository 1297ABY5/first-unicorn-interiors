Interior Design Division

Spaces Designed Around You

Dubai is a city of extraordinary homes and ambitious commercial spaces. Whether you are furnishing a new penthouse on Palm Jumeirah, redesigning a family villa in Arabian Ranches, or fitting out a restaurant in DIFC, the interiors need to work as hard as they look.

Our interiors division brings together designers, project managers and procurement specialists under one roof. We handle every stage — from initial concept and 3D visualisation through material sourcing, manufacturing coordination and final installation. No sub-contractors. No gaps between design intent and built reality.

With over 800 projects delivered across Dubai's most discerning communities, we understand the nuances that make a space genuinely liveable: how light behaves in a south-facing lounge, which stone holds up in a high-traffic hallway, and why a kitchen island needs to be 50mm lower than you think. These are the details that separate good design from great design.
