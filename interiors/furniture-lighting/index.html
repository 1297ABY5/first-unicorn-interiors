The Challenge

Why Furniture Sourcing Is Harder Than It Looks

Dubai has no shortage of furniture showrooms. From the sprawling galleries of Sheikh Zayed Road to the branded boutiques of D3 and the wholesale warehouses of Al Quoz, there is an overwhelming amount of choice. But more choice does not mean better outcomes. Most homeowners spend months visiting showrooms, collecting business cards and comparing prices — only to end up with a collection of pieces that do not quite work together, cost more than they budgeted, and arrive at different times with no coordinated installation.

The pricing model of traditional retail furniture in Dubai is also stacked against the buyer. A sofa that leaves the Italian factory at EUR 3,000 passes through an importer, a distributor and a showroom before reaching you at AED 30,000 or more. Each layer adds margin. Each layer adds time. And none of those layers adds any design value — they are simply logistical intermediaries taking a cut.

Lighting presents an even more complex challenge. A living room needs ambient light for relaxed evenings, task lighting for reading, accent lighting for artwork and architectural features, and possibly decorative fixtures that serve as design statements in their own right. Getting this layered scheme right requires understanding lumen output, colour temperature, beam angles, dimming compatibility and the physical relationship between fixtures and the space they illuminate. It is technical work that most furniture showrooms are simply not equipped to advise on. Our team combines interior design sensibility with technical lighting knowledge to deliver schemes that look beautiful and function perfectly — then we source every fixture, handle the electrical coordination and install it all ourselves.

What We Deliver

Furniture & Lighting Services

Direct Sourcing

We purchase directly from factories and authorised manufacturers in Italy, Spain, Portugal, Turkey and the UAE. No showroom markups, no distributor margins. You get the same quality at 20 to 40 percent less than retail, with full manufacturer warranties and verified authenticity on every piece.

Custom Pieces

When standard catalogues do not have what you need, we design and manufacture bespoke furniture to your exact specifications. Dining tables sized for your room, sofas in your chosen fabric and dimensions, shelving systems that fit awkward alcoves, and statement pieces that exist nowhere else. All produced by specialist workshops we have worked with for years.

Lighting Design

We design complete lighting schemes with layered ambient, task, accent and decorative lighting. Every fixture is specified for output, colour temperature and dimming compatibility. We handle the electrical layout coordination with your contractor or our own team, and source fixtures from leading European and specialist manufacturers.

Installation Service

Our team delivers, assembles, positions and installs every item. Furniture is unpacked, assembled, levelled and placed exactly as designed. Lighting fixtures are wired, mounted and aimed. We remove all packaging, clean the space and make final adjustments on the spot. You do not lift a finger.

Warranty Support

Every product we supply comes with the manufacturer's warranty, and we act as your local point of contact for any claims. If a mechanism fails, a fabric tears prematurely or a light fixture malfunctions, you call us — not a factory in Italy. We handle the claim, arrange the replacement and coordinate the swap.

Interior Styling

Once the furniture and lighting are in place, our stylists add the finishing layer: cushions, throws, rugs, artwork, decorative objects, tableware and greenery. This is what transforms a furnished space into a home that feels lived-in and loved. We source every accessory and style it in person during the final installation day.

Common Questions

Furniture & Lighting FAQ

How do you source furniture without retail markups?

We buy direct from manufacturers and their authorised distributors, bypassing the retail showroom chain entirely. This means no showroom rent, no sales commission and no multi-layer markup. We pass the savings on to you while still offering the same brands and quality you would find in Dubai's top furniture showrooms. On average, our clients save 20 to 40 percent compared to retail pricing.

Can you create custom furniture pieces?

Yes. We work with specialist manufacturers in Italy, Turkey and the UAE to produce bespoke furniture to your exact specifications. This includes dining tables, sofas, beds, storage units, shelving systems, outdoor furniture and one-off statement pieces. We handle the design, technical drawings, material selection, manufacturing supervision and delivery.

What lighting services do you offer?

We provide complete lighting design and supply. This includes lighting layout planning, fixture selection, dimming and control system specification, decorative and architectural lighting sourcing, and full installation. We work with leading European lighting brands as well as specialist manufacturers for custom chandeliers, LED profiles and landscape lighting.

How long does furniture delivery take from Europe?

Standard production and shipping from Italian and European manufacturers typically takes 8 to 14 weeks. Some brands offer express production in 4 to 6 weeks at a premium. We factor these lead times into the project programme from the start and coordinate delivery with installation schedules so everything arrives when needed. For urgent requirements, we also source from UAE-based manufacturers and stock items.

Do you handle delivery and installation?

Yes — this is a turnkey service. We handle shipping, customs clearance, warehousing (if items arrive before the space is ready), delivery to your home and professional installation. Our team assembles, positions and levels every piece. We also handle any protective wrapping removal, packaging disposal and post-installation adjustments.
