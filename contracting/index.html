Construction & Contracting

From Foundation to Handover — One Team, One Standard

Dubai's construction landscape demands more than blueprints and ambition. It demands a contractor who understands the municipality process, the structural realities of building in desert conditions, and the relentless pace required to deliver on time.

First Unicorn's Contracting division handles everything from ground-up villa builds to mid-rise developments up to G+10. We bring our own structural engineers, MEP specialists, and project managers — no subcontractor roulette, no finger-pointing between trades. One team carries the project from mobilisation through to certificate of completion.

Whether you are building a family home in Arabian Ranches or a mixed-use tower in Business Bay, we deliver the same thing: transparent pricing, meticulous engineering, and a building that performs decades after the scaffolding comes down.

Our Process

How We Deliver

1

Feasibility & Design

Site assessment, soil testing, structural design, and full authority submissions. We establish scope, budget, and timeline before breaking ground.

2

Approvals & Permits

Municipality submissions, building permits, NOCs, and utility connections. Our approvals team knows every authority requirement in Dubai.

3

Construction

Mobilisation, structural works, MEP rough-in, finishing, and external works. Weekly progress reports and milestone-based payments keep everything transparent.

4

Handover

Snagging, final inspections, certificate of completion, and handover documentation. One-year defects liability period on all works.
